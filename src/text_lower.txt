0: sing joyfully to the lord you righteous it is fitting for the upright to praise him
1: praise the lord with the harp make music to him on the ten-stringed lyre
2: sing to him a new song play skillfully and shout for joy
3: for the word of the lord is right and true he is faithful in all he does
4: the lord loves righteousness and justice the earth is full of his unfailing love
5: by the word of the lord were the heavens made their starry host by the breath of his mouth
6: he gathers the waters of the sea into jars  he puts the deep into storehouses
7: let all the earth fear the lord let all the people of the world revere him
8: for he spoke and it came to be he commanded and it stood firm
9: the lord foils the plans of the nations he thwarts the purposes of the peoples
10: but the plans of the lord stand firm forever the purposes of his heart through all generations
11: blessed is the nation whose god is the lord the people he chose for his inheritance
12: from heaven the lord looks down and sees all mankind
13: from his dwelling place he watches all who live on earth
14: he who forms the hearts of all who considers everything they do
15: no king is saved by the size of his army no warrior escapes by his great strength
16: a horse is a vain hope for deliverance despite all its great strength it cannot save
17: but the eyes of the lord are on those who fear him on those whose hope is in his unfailing love
18: to deliver them from death and keep them alive in famine
19: we wait in hope for the lord he is our help and our shield
20: in him our hearts rejoice for we trust in his holy name
21: may your unfailing love rest upon us o lord even as we put our hope in you
